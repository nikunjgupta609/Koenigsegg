Koenigsegg Agera

Koenigsegg Hypercar

The Agera was designed to be a lightweight, high-performance car, with a focus on aerodynamics and speed. It is powered by a twin-turbocharged 5.0-liter V8 engine that produces up to 1,360 horsepower and 1,011 lb-ft of torque. The car is capable of accelerating from 0 to 60 miles per hour in less than three seconds and can reach a top speed of over 270 miles per hour.

Swedish Automaker Koenigsegg

The car is named after the Swedish word "agera," which means "to act" or "to perform. One of the most unique features of the Agera is its "dihedral synchro-helix" doors, which open upwards and rotate outwards as they rise. The car also features a lightweight carbon fiber chassis, active aerodynamics, and a custom-built transmission that can shift gears in less than 100 milliseconds.

Koenigsegg Born to drive.

Koenigsegg produced several variants of the Agera, including the Agera R, Agera S, Agera RS, and Agera Final. The Agera RS set a new top speed record for production cars in 2017, reaching a speed of 277.9 miles per hour. Only a few dozen Ageras were produced, making it a rare and exclusive car.
