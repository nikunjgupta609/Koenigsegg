Koenigsegg Jesko

Koenigsegg Your Desire For Speed

The car is named after the father of Koenigsegg founder Christian von Koenigsegg, Jesko von Koenigsegg. The Jesko is powered by a twin-turbocharged 5.0-liter V8 engine that produces up to 1,600 horsepower and 1,106 lb-ft of torque when running on E85 biofuel. The car is also equipped with Koenigsegg's Light Speed Transmission (LST), a nine-speed multi-clutch gearbox that can shift at record-breaking speeds.

Bold Moves Koenigsegg

The Jesko features advanced aerodynamics, including a large front splitter, active rear wing, and underbody diffuser, that generate over 2,200 pounds of downforce at high speeds. The car also has a unique and innovative suspension system, called Triplex, which uses three dampers per corner to improve handling and stability.

Koenigsegg Aerodynamic Body

The Jesko is designed to be both luxurious and high-performance, with a bespoke interior that can be customized to the owner's preferences. Only 125 units of the Jesko will be produced, with each car being built to order and tailored to the customer's specific needs and desires.
