Gemera

An all-new Koenigsegg


Ultimate performance has belonged to the world of two-seaters with very limited luggage space – until now. The Gemera is the world’s first Mega-GT and Koenigsegg’s first four-seater. Extreme megacar meets spacious interior and ultimate environmental consciousnes. It also has an innovative and eco-friendly powertrain that uses a small three-cylinder engine and three electric motors, allowing it to achieve high power output while reducing emissions. Additionally, it features advanced technology, such as a fully digital cockpit and an active suspension system, making it a unique and futuristic vehicle.

Jesko

The ultimate driving machine


The Jesko delivers more power, more downforce and more technology in the ultimate track-focused car for the road. The Jesko Absolut delivers its high-speed stability optimized aero features while also having daily drive characteristics and luggage space. The car also features a range of other high-tech materials, including titanium exhaust components and custom-made carbon fiber wheels, all of which contribute to its exceptional performance and unique character.
